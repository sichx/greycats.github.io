OUR WORK

  • Threadflip

    Threadflip is pioneering a new social shopping experience by offering women a simple way to convert their closets into a dynamic boutique-like experience, connecting buyers with sellers, and capturing the collaborative energy of shopping with friends. Threadflip has been featured in the iOS app store on multiple occasions.

  • Flares

    Flares is a new mobile messaging service that enables you to shoot visual signals to your friends and family to let them know exactly where you are - when it matters to them the most. Use Flares to indicate a meeting spot, let everyone know you’re running late, coordinate drop-off and pick-up locations.

  • Tidepool

    Tidepool helps you keep your mind sharp with a daily training program. Play scientifically designed games for training and insights, track your performance, and challenge your friends.

  • Gemshare

    GemShare is a network for personal recommendations for the local services we all need to keep life running smoothly. Get real recommendations from people you know and trust: friends, friends of friends, and groups you create and join.
